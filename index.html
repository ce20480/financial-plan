4. Pension (SIPP)

Personal pension contributions extend your basic rate band, moving income out of 40% tax. [GOV.UK: Pension tax relief]

How tax relief works

For every GBP 100 you want in your pension:

You pay (net contribution)GBP 80
HMRC adds 20% basic rate relief (automatic)+ GBP 20
In your pension potGBP 100
Higher rate relief (via Self Assessment, on amount above GBP 50,270)+ 20% refund
